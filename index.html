My UX Skills

User Interface

Here I focus on what users or real people might need to do on a particular
site and ensure that the interface has easily assesible designs and elements to
meet their goals! This is my favorite skill because I enjoy catchng users attention through
different design elements!

User Experience

I try to have a deep understand of what a users needs, what they value, and what
capabilities they have when creating an application. Conducting user testing throughout
the whole process helps both the designer and the user have a better experience!

and many other resources to create something that is fully functional and gives me as the designer more
flexibility to do what I want!
